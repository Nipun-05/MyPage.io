My Family

I have a sister. She is Nabha and is elder to me by seven years. I call her "bhoot" (it means ghost in Marathi) as she loooks like one everytime she wakes up. Super lazy btw. Will never do her work on time but is helpful and good. I always trouble her. But she does get that for troubling me to do just seriously small tasks like turning up the fan in the room even though I'm aroung 25 feet from it.


My Ma


My ma is a very sweet, sentimental lady. She has been through some serious stuff. My dad left us for his second wife when I was 6. So ma had to raise us two herself with only her salary as dad would not send any money. She tries her best to make sure that we don't miss dad. She's old now! 50 YEARS! Well, she is a great cook, tailor, firefighter,etc. Love you ma!


My Grandparents


In Marathi (my native language) we calll grandfather "Baba" and grandmother "Aaji", that's how I will refer to them hence. Baba and Aaji are really good. Baba does all he can to keep us happy. He busts his bottom to do everything for everyone in the family. Aaji stays home and does all the cooking and stuff. She is good, too.

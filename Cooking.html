My Cooking


Hey everyone! it's your friendly wannabe Masterchef!


My Choice of Feild


To be frank, cooking was just a hobby until now. Sure, I had a lot of passion for it, but never saw at it as a proffesion. I always wanted to be an automobile engineer. Cars and I were close as all boys are. I loved them so much that I would ask for car toys, stationary,etc. But, ma told me that till 2025, not just India but the entire world was to be cleared of vehicled running on deisel and petrol and switch to electric or solar cars. This meant that even if I was the topper of my class, I would have a lesser scope tham an electrical engineer. So, I switched fields!


Why Did I Do Ths?


I did this because, even though cars were my favourite, I always had a specialbond with food. Yes, you'd be thinking of me eating it, but no; this bond was different. It felt as if not just me but the food was talking to me. So I heard. I heard and heard and felt like I need to do this. THIS is my passion. And that is how I switched from automobile engineer to a chef.


My food
